BRICS Summit 2024: What's Brewing in Kazan?

So, the BRICS Summit 2024 is kicking off in Kazan, Russia, and it’s already got people talking. The big players—Brazil, Russia, India, China, and South Africa—are back together for their 16th annual meetup. But this time, it’s not just the usual suspects. With new members like Saudi Arabia, Iran, Egypt, and the UAE joining the BRICS party, it's like they’re expanding the guest list to make it the hottest club in global diplomacy.

The theme this year? "Strengthening Multilateralism for Just Global Development and Security." Sounds fancy, right? What it really means is that these leaders are going to chat about everything from economic cooperation and global security to dedollarization—yes, they’re trying to ditch the dollar! Russia, as the host, is pushing this pretty hard, hoping to shake up the global financial system with the introduction of BRICS Pay, a sort of SWIFT alternative for smoother international payments.

Now, India’s PM Modi is there, of course, and he's expected to have some serious one-on-one time with his buddies Putin and Xi Jinping. This is Modi’s second visit to Russia this year—seems like he can’t get enough of the borscht (or maybe the geopolitics). With these heavyweights in the same room, we can expect some interesting discussions, particularly on how these countries can navigate a world that’s, well, a little unpredictable at the moment​.

In short, it’s a summit worth keeping an eye on. Whether it’s shaking up the global financial system or figuring out how to work together despite their differences, BRICS is definitely making moves. And as always, with world leaders in one room, expect a few surprises along the way!
